   Availability of Customer Systems
   ================================
   
   The :guilabel:`Availability` panel on the customer dashboard displays the percentage of time that the
   customer's systems are available for S3 transactions. 
   
   When you hover your mouse over a graphic, you see the actual uptime percentage of the system.
